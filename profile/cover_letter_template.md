# Cover Letter Style Guide

## Tone
Professional but conversational. Confident, not arrogant.
Avoid buzzwords like "passionate", "rockstar", "ninja".

## Structure
1. Opening: one sentence stating the role and a hook about why this company
2. Middle: 2 short paragraphs — one on relevant experience, one on fit
3. Close: brief, direct call to action

## Always include
- A specific detail about the company that shows I researched them
- One concrete achievement with a number if possible

## Never include
- "I am writing to apply for..."
- Generic enthusiasm ("I have always been passionate about...")
- More than 3 paragraphs total

## Length
250–350 words maximum. Short is better than long.
